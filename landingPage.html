About Us

Welcome to FURFECT CARE, the ultimate web-based pet care management system designed to simplify and enhance pet ownership.


Our platform provides pet owners with the tools they need to efficiently track and manage their pets' health, appointments, and daily needs. With intuitive features and a user-friendly interface, you can ensure that your furry friends receive the best care possible, all in one convenient place.

Features

RECORD MANAGEMENT
Efficiently organizes and maintains essential records related to your pet's health and well-being. This includes:

  • Health records
  • Medication records
  • Vaccination alerts

APPOINTMENT MANAGEMENT
Streamlines the process of scheduling and managing appointments for your pet, making it easier to stay on top of their care:

  • Sends Reminder
  • Scheduling

DIETARY MANAGEMENT
Helps you maintain a healthy diet for your pet by providing tools to manage their feeding routine:

  • Sends Feeding Reminder

PET PROFILE MANAGEMENT
Centralizes all important information about your pet in one convenient location:

  • Photos

WELLNESS MANAGEMENT
Supports your pet's overall well-being by promoting regular exercise and activity:

  • Sends Reminder for exercise
